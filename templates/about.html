Disc Golf Statistics App

Our Disc Golf Statistics App is a web application designed to display disc golf-related data, including player scores, courses, rounds, and scorecards.

This application was made by 4 friends as a school project, because we all love to play disc golf this was no brainer application to do for us because now it's easier to browse our scores.
